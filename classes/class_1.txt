Class: Menu item

name (string)
price (float)
timeToPrepare (integer)
ingredients (array)
spicy (boolean)
glutenFree (boolean)

putOnSale (multiplies price by .75, then changes price to that amount)
calculateTip (multiples price by .2)
addIngredient (appends an ingredient to ingredients array)
makeSpicy (changes spicy to true and appends hot chili sauce to ingredients array)
